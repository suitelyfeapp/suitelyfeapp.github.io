FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

HOW ARE YOU DIFFERENT FROM OTHER HOTEL BOOKING APPS/WEBSITES?

Well, simply put, people who are geographically distanced from Singapore are unable to book hotel rooms through our app. Why should locals who want to go on staycations compete with traveller for the same room? Following this logic, we are thus able to offer exclusive prices for locals, giving you a greater value for your buck.

Why can you provide such low prices?

With contracts held by our hand-picked hotels, we are able to obtain their empty rooms and monetize them with discounts of at least 20% compared to other booking platforms.

...Really? How can you ensure that you have the lowest price guarantee?

We compare these prices with the lowest price on the Internet, and guarantee that we have the lowest prices just for you. There’s just no need to worry when you’re booking through our platform - we do all the work for you!

What if I find a cheaper rate for the exact same room on another platform?

We try to ensure that our rooms are of the lowest prices in the market. We sincerely apologize if you find the same room for a cheaper price. If that happens, we’d return you SuiteLyfe credits doubling the difference that you paid on SuiteLyfe. See terms and conditions for details.

What hotels are on your app?

We have hotels that range from 3-star boutiques to 5-star hotels. We are always in the process of gathering more hotels, so please do be patient with us if you don’t see your favorite hotel on board (we are probably in the process of negotiation with them)!

Are suites the only available room type on your app?

Nope! In order to cater to a wider range of audiences, we do have a variety of different hotel room types, including single rooms, double rooms, and of course, suites!

Can I book a room a month in advance?

Sorry, you can’t! Our rooms have a lead time of one week, meaning that you can only book seven days in advance up till the day itself.

Do the rooms get cheaper as it becomes more ‘last-minute’?

Technically, yes, since same-day rooms are usually lower in demand. The seven-day booking window that we have implemented is the optimal window that we feel offers the most competitive rates, so not to worry, you will still get outstanding prices by booking anytime within the seven-day window.

What type of room will I get?

You can choose the number of people that you would be housing in the room. Depending on that and the hotel’s assignment, the hotel room type will differ. But rest assured, all the rooms offered on our platform are of great quality!

Can I book a room in overseas hotels?

Sorry, you can’t! Our app currently only offers hotels in Singapore (hey, it’s a great place to be!).

Must I be in Singapore to book a room?

Yes, you must be in Singapore to book a room. Our app is localized, meaning that you can only book local hotels if you are in the same geographical region.
